About This Project

My Reading Journey is a personal book tracker web app that helps users manage, rate, and reflect on the books theyâ€™ve read. Whether you're a casual reader or an avid bookworm, this platform makes it simple to organize your reading experience.


Features

  • User authentication with secure login and signup.
  • Add book entries with details like title, author, genre, description, start/finish dates, and a cover image.
  • Edit or delete books details anytime.
  • Rate books out of 5 stars and add detailed descriptions to it.
  • Track when you started and finished reading
  • Organize books by title, genre, and author
  • View your collection as book cards on the homepage and click for full details.
  • Supports light/dark themes for better experience.

How It Works

  1. Sign up or log in to your account.
  2. Add a book (only the title is required at first, rest can be filled in later).
  3. Your books are displayed on the homepage as cards.
  4. Click action buttons at bottom card to view details, edit, or delete it.

Building Technologies

This project is built using:

  • Backend: Flask, SQLAlchemy, WTForms, Werkzeug
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Jinja Templates
  • Database: SQLite (default, lightweight and easy to set up, also free)

The app follows a simple CRUD system (Create, Read, Update, Delete) for managing books.
